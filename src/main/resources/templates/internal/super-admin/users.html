Role guidance

  • SUPER_ADMIN has full platform control.
  • CONTENT_MANAGER manages catalog, blog, and media.
  • OPS_MANAGER handles bookings, orders, and applications.
  • ADMIN is the compatibility staff role used across internal access checks.
  • External roles keep the account outside the internal staff directory while remaining active in the platform.
